This portfolio showcases some of the projects I have created in Power BI throughout the years. I am extremely passionate about data and data visualization. Puerto Rico Homelessness Survey 2022

  • The data for this dashboard was obtained by interviewing over 300 homeless with the purpose of better understanding their stories. It is crucial to have extensive experience with developing, maintaining and managing of Power BI architecture to create effective visuals for such a sensitive subject.

Puerto Rico Permits Dashboard

  • Permit data is downloaded monthly using scripts uploaded to Google Cloud Console and uploaded to our SQL Database. This dashboard pulls queries and creates relational tables to create the metrics shown.

Infrastructure Dashboard by Area

  • Over 50 geographic variables are used to create various the Infrastructure Index per area. The variables range from electrical lines, to universities, and even luminosity. A strong understanding of various industries, including insurance, was required to build this dashboard.

ETI Project Managment System

  • This dashboard strives to compare productivity among teams in a company. Over 30 metrics are calculated from various sources. Using this dashboard I have gained valuable inter-personal and communication skills by setting up meetings to better understand each division and employee at my current job. Division's definition of productivity can vary greatly and thus working closely with management proved to be essential for this project.
